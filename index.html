About Us

We take pride in our dedicated team, consisting of skilled dental hygienists, dental assistants, lab technicians, and office staff. With a genuine sense of care and concern, we strive to deliver the best customer experience possible. Our primary objective is to ensure that every appointment is not only comfortable but also relieves any discomfort and helps you achieve the smile you've always desired.

At the helm of our clinic is Dr. J. P. Mishra, the esteemed founder.
